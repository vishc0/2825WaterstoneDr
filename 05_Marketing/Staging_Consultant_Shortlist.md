## Suggested Phone Script

Hello, I am preparing a premium executive home in South Forsyth / Cumming for sale very soon. The home has a creek lot, double decks, updated interiors, a finished basement with full in-law suite and kitchen, media room, game room, office with glass doors, and a three-car garage.

We are close to completion and may have limited time before photography. I need a fast staging consultation focused on highest-ROI listing preparation, occupied-home styling, accessory recommendations, and possibly selective rental pieces.

Can you support a quick turnaround in Cumming, Georgia? If so, I would like help prioritizing the foyer, living room, kitchen, office, primary suite, basement/in-law suite, media/game areas, and decks.

## Questions to Ask Each Consultant

- Are you available for a consultation within the next few days?
- Do you serve Cumming / South Forsyth?
- Do you handle occupied-home staging, not only vacant staging?
- Can you provide accessories, bedding, art, pillows, greenery, and outdoor deck styling pieces?
- Can you help prepare specifically for real estate photography?
- Can you return on photo day for final styling?
- What is your consultation fee?
- What would partial staging cost for the highest-impact rooms?
- Do you offer pay-at-close or agent-billed options?
- Which three changes would most improve buyer perception and listing photos?

## Selection Criteria

Choose the consultant who can best deliver:

- Fastest availability
- Strongest luxury listing experience
- Clear occupied-home staging process
- Ability to support photo-day polish
- Good understanding of relocation and executive buyers
- Practical focus on ROI rather than over-staging
- Specific plan for basement, in-law suite, and outdoor decks

## Notes

Because the timeline is tight, contact all first five options the same day. Do not wait for one response before calling the next. Ask for the earliest possible consultation and whether they can work from photos or video first if an in-person visit is delayed.
