## Buyer lenses

- **Relocation:** finished condition, office/library, guest suite, second kitchen and community amenities.
- **Work from home:** main office/library plus main-level guest bedroom that can serve as a second office when not hosting guests.
- **Extended stay:** kitchen, bath, bedroom option and deck without describing the level as a separate legal dwelling.
- **Entertaining:** formal dining, island kitchen, terrace multipurpose room, game/flex room, decks and outdoor flow.
- **Outdoor living:** professional landscaping, fruit trees, seasonal outlooks, creek sound and community trails/amenities.

## Verify before writing or contracting

- Measurements and above/below-grade treatment.
- Room classification, egress and permits for flexible rooms.
- HOA terms, event/parking rules and community amenities.
- School assignments and travel routes.
- Creek adjacency/frontage, seasonal views and distance claims.
- Owner-described 150-person gathering, fireworks and fruiting history as experience—not occupancy or performance guarantees.
