## Best buyer matches

### Executive relocation / corporate transfer

Lead with: finished condition, office/library, guest suite, second kitchen, substantial total area, GA-400 orientation and community amenities.

Ask: move timing, commute destinations, documentation needs and whether a relocation advisor needs a formal packet.

### Two-parent work-from-home household

Lead with: main office/library plus a main-level guest-bedroom office option, private upper rooms, flexible media room and open kitchen/living reconnection point.

Avoid promising soundproofing; describe privacy and separation of spaces.

### Multigenerational / extended-stay buyer

Lead with: three connected-but-distinct zones, second kitchen, full bath, lower bedroom, deck and storage/fitness/activity options.

Verify: access, egress, permits, utilities, HOA rules, bedroom classification and whether the buyer needs a legal independent dwelling.

### Entertainer / community host

Lead with: formal dining, island kitchen, terrace multipurpose room, game/flex room, separate lower-deck door, double decks and indoor/outdoor flow.

Owner-reported experience: a 150-person indoor/outdoor gathering. Treat as event history, not permitted occupancy or fire-capacity approval.

### Luxury lifestyle / privacy-oriented buyer

Lead with: primary retreat, custom closet, quartz bath, replaced flooring, refreshed decks, professional landscaping, fruit trees, seasonal outlooks and community setting.

Use “estate-scale flexibility” or “estate feeling within a community,” not an unsupported legal/category claim of “estate.”

## Questions agents should answer before a buyer writes

- Which room count is in both FMLS and GAMLS? Current official count: 6 bedrooms / 5 full baths.
- How are the second-floor media/flex room and basement game/flex room classified?
- What supports the 6,377 sq ft total and above/below-grade breakdown?
- What permits, final inspections or documentation exist for basement finish, kitchen/bath work and decks?
- What are the roof, HVAC, water-heater and major-system ages/warranties?
- What does the HOA include and restrict?
- What are current school assignments and route times?
- Is Dave’s Creek legally adjacent/frontage, or is it an outlook/sound feature?
- What event, parking, fire-capacity or HOA rules apply to large gatherings?

## Recommended document packet

Ask the listing representative for the current versions of:

1. Appraisal and measurement/floor-plan support.
2. Upgrade chronology, receipts, warranties and contractor information where available.
3. Permit search and final-inspection information.
4. Roof, HVAC, tankless-water-heater and termite documentation.
5. HOA declaration, budget, inclusions, restrictions and amenity confirmation.
6. School-assignment source and route notes.
7. Showing instructions and offer-submission process.
