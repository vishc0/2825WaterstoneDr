## 3. Main Topics Covered

### Topic A: Kitchen Cabinet Hardware Selection

#### Hardware Board Reviewed

A Richelieu hardware sample board was provided showing:

* Matte black pulls
* Brushed nickel pulls
* Champagne brass / satin brass pulls
* Various knobs

#### Kitchen Conditions Reviewed

* White cabinets
* Black granite countertops
* Stainless appliances
* Warm wood-tone LVP floors
* White subway backsplash

#### Initial Ranking

1. Matte Black Bar Pulls
2. Champagne Brass Pulls
3. Brushed Nickel Pulls

Reasoning:

* Black provides strongest contrast.
* Photographs well.
* Matches black granite.

#### Additional Review After Hardware Photos

Photos showed existing round gold-toned knobs.

Conclusion:

* Existing knobs appear to be:

  * Champagne Bronze
  * Satin Brass
  * Brushed Brass family
* Not polished brass.

#### Revised Recommendation

After reviewing the full home:

Preferred finish:

* Champagne Bronze / Satin Brass

Reasoning:

* Coordinates with warm flooring.
* Softens black granite.
* Provides upscale transitional appearance.
* Less common than black hardware.
* Creates warmer executive-home aesthetic.

Recommended configuration:

* Keep champagne bronze knobs on doors.
* Add matching champagne bronze pulls on drawers.
* Use longer pulls on larger drawers.

### Topic E: Lighting Strategy

#### Proposal Reviewed

Idea:

* Install smart recessed lighting throughout main floor.
* Multi-color RGB capable.

#### Recommendation

Do NOT make RGB lighting the primary lighting strategy.

Reasoning:

* Executive buyers associate colored ceilings with:

  * Gaming rooms
  * Entertainment spaces
  * Tech novelty

Not typically associated with luxury homes.

#### Recommended Lighting Approach

Use:

* Smart tunable white recessed lighting

Temperature range:

* 2700K–5000K

Preferred settings:

| Area        | Temperature |
| ----------- | ----------- |
| Foyer       | 3000K       |
| Dining      | 2700K       |
| Kitchen     | 3000K–3500K |
| Family Room | 2700K       |
| Hallways    | 3000K       |
| Office      | 3500K       |
| Media Room  | 2700K       |
