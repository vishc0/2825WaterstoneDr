# 2825 Waterstone – Smart Home & Luxury Toilet Upgrade Summary

## Overall Objective

Increase buyer perception of a **modern executive luxury home** while maintaining strong ROI and avoiding over-investment in features that do not significantly increase sale price.

---

# Smart Home Recommendations

## HIGH ROI Upgrades

### Smart Lighting

Install smart switches (preferred over smart bulbs) for:

* Front porch lights
* Garage coach lights
* Entry foyer chandelier
* Kitchen lighting
* Dining room lighting
* Rear deck lighting

Recommended:

* TP-Link Tapo
* TP-Link Kasa

Benefits:

* Sunset/sunrise schedules
* Vacation mode
* Google Home compatibility
* Apple Home compatibility

---

### Smart Dimmers

Install smart dimmers for:

* Kitchen recessed lights
* Kitchen pendants
* Dining room chandelier
* Foyer chandelier

Buyer Impact:

* High

---

### Smart Garage Door

Recommended:

* myQ
* Meross

Buyer Perception:

* High

---

### Smart Lock

Recommended:

* Schlage Encode Plus
* Yale Assure Lock 2

Buyer Perception:

* Very High

---

### Mesh WiFi (instead of Extenders)

Current State:

* Wired Ethernet throughout home
* Router located in basement

Recommended:

* TP-Link Deco
* Eero

Use Ethernet backhaul.

Buyer Perception:

* High
* Demonstrates premium networking infrastructure

---

### Existing Smart Features

Already Installed:

* Ecobee thermostats
* Wired Ethernet in all rooms
* Central networking infrastructure

These are valuable selling points.

---

### SimpliSafe

Recommendation:

Install only if already owned.

Reason:

* Buyers rarely pay more for security systems.
* Nice feature but lower ROI than lighting, locks, and networking.

# Toilet Upgrade Strategy

## Key Insight

Luxury perception comes from:

* Modern design
* Clean lines
* Hidden trapways
* One-piece construction
* Consistency across all bathrooms

NOT from simply having smart toilets everywhere.
