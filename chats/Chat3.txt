### Topic B: Buyer Psychology Analysis

Key buyer thought process:

Pros:

* Large home
* Move-in ready appearance
* Updated finishes
* Multigenerational capability
* Basement amenities
* Outdoor living space
* Privacy
* Creek setting

Likely objections:

* Age of HVAC systems
* Age of roof
* Builder-grade closets
* Kitchen countertops if not upgraded

Important insight:

Buyers do not primarily purchase square footage.

They purchase:

* Lifestyle
* Privacy
* Outdoor experiences
* Family functionality
* Emotional connection
