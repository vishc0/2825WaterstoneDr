* **Buyer segment:** Executive / relocation / high-income buyers

## 3. Main Topics Covered

### Topic A: Scent Strategy for Home Staging

**Core principle:**

* Use **subtle, consistent, luxury scent profile**
* Avoid strong or mixed fragrances
* Avoid masking odors — eliminate them instead

**Target scent profile:**

* White Tea
* Bergamot
* Eucalyptus
* Cedar / light wood
* Fresh linen / clean notes

**Positioning logic:**

* Mimics luxury hotels and builder model homes
* Appeals to broad buyer demographics
* Signals cleanliness, quality, and maintenance

---

### Topic B: Room-by-Room Scent Placement

**Foyer / Entry**

* White tea, bergamot
* Purpose: strong first impression

**Living Room**

* White tea + cedar / sandalwood
* Purpose: where buyers spend most time

**Kitchen**

* Minimal scent
* Natural cues: lemon, light coffee
* Avoid artificial or food scents

**Primary Bedroom**

* Light lavender / linen / eucalyptus
* Purpose: calm, spa-like environment

**Primary Bathroom**

* Eucalyptus + mint / sea salt
* Purpose: luxury spa feeling

**Basement**

* Cedar / fresh linen
* Priority: odor removal first

**Outdoor**

* No scent — allow natural creek/air experience

---

### Topic C: Diffuser Type Strategy

**Preferred:**

* Reed diffusers (low maintenance, consistent)
* Smart diffusers (optional, controlled scenting)

**Avoid:**

* Candles (inconsistent, safety concerns)
* Strong plug-ins (can feel artificial)

---

### Topic D: Budget Product Selection (Under $30)

Focus: **reed diffusers with sticks (bottle-based)**

Selected products across Amazon / Walmart / Target / World Market equivalents.

---

## 4. Decisions Already Made

* **Decision:** Use reed diffusers instead of candles

  * **Reason:** Consistent scent, safer, more professional staging
  * **Impact:** Improves perceived quality and avoids artificial feel

* **Decision:** Use a unified scent profile (White Tea / Bergamot / Eucalyptus)

  * **Reason:** Avoids scent clashes and creates brand-like experience
  * **Impact:** Higher buyer comfort, longer showing duration

* **Decision:** Keep kitchen mostly fragrance-free

  * **Reason:** Buyers distrust artificial food smells
  * **Impact:** Builds trust, reduces suspicion

---
