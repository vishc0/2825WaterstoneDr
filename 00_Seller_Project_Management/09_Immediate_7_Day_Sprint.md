# Immediate 7-Day Sprint

Purpose: Sequence the next week so the home moves toward photography and listing without scattered effort.

## Day 1: Alignment and Calls

- [ ] Confirm target photography date with realtor.
- [ ] Confirm target listing date with realtor.
- [ ] Contact top five staging consultants the same day.
- [ ] Ask realtor for final photographer recommendation and availability.
- [ ] Ask realtor for CMA and pricing strategy against appraisal.
- [ ] Ask realtor whether to order pre-listing inspection, HVAC, roof, and termite reports.

## Day 2: Documentation

- [ ] Collect appraisal PDF and appraiser remarks.
- [ ] Collect HOA documents.
- [ ] Collect renovation receipts.
- [ ] Collect HVAC records.
- [ ] Collect roof records.
- [ ] Collect termite records.
- [ ] Collect utility costs.
- [ ] Create missing document list.

## Day 3: House Walkthrough

- [ ] Walk front exterior and entry.
- [ ] Walk kitchen and living area.
- [ ] Walk office.
- [ ] Walk primary suite.
- [ ] Walk basement/in-law suite.
- [ ] Walk media/game rooms.
- [ ] Walk decks.
- [ ] Walk garage.
- [ ] Mark defects that affect photos or buyer confidence.

## Day 4: Repair and Upgrade Decisions

- [ ] Finalize hardware/fixture/lighting decisions.
- [ ] Decide whether any ceiling fans need replacement.
- [ ] Decide whether under-cabinet lighting is worth doing.
- [ ] Confirm deck repair/stain/cleaning status.
- [ ] Confirm fireplace/TV wire cleanup.
- [ ] Confirm remaining handyman tasks.

## Day 5: Staging Plan

- [ ] Choose staging consultant or fallback self-staging plan.
- [ ] Decide full vs partial staging.
- [ ] Prioritize foyer, living, kitchen, office, primary suite, basement/in-law suite, and decks.
- [ ] Decide what furniture or decor must be removed.
- [ ] Decide what accessories must be purchased or rented.

## Day 6: Cleaning and Photo Prep

- [ ] Schedule deep cleaning.
- [ ] Schedule window/glass cleaning.
- [ ] Schedule landscaping refresh.
- [ ] Clear counters.
- [ ] Remove personal photos.
- [ ] Hide cords.
- [ ] Prepare decks.
- [ ] Prepare basement lighting.

## Day 7: Marketing Foundation

- [ ] Draft upgrade sheet.
- [ ] Draft buyer FAQ.
- [ ] Draft property story.
- [ ] Choose website/due-diligence room format.
- [ ] Start buyer-agent packet.
- [ ] Prepare photographer shot priorities.
- [ ] Prepare QR code plan.
