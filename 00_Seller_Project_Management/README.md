Working rule: complete tasks in order unless a vendor schedule forces a change. Focus first on items that improve photography, buyer confidence, and inspection/appraisal risk.

Current project emphasis from the saved chats and appraiser notes:

- Staging is likely higher ROI than additional broad renovation at this stage.
- Decks, creek sounds, basement/in-law suite, office, and executive lifestyle positioning are core differentiators.
- Appraisal support is a constraint; keep pricing, comps, upgrades, and buyer-confidence documents organized.
- Roof, HVAC, termite, basement permit/status, and service history should be documented before buyers use them as negotiation leverage.
