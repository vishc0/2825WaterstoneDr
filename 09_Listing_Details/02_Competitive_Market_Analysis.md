# Competitive Market Analysis

Property: 2825 Waterstone Drive, Cumming, GA 30041  
Analysis date: July 12, 2026

## Bottom line

The **$1,150,000 list price is market-defensible but not conservative**. It is supported by a same-subdivision $1.205M sale from May 2025, a June 2026 Windermere sale at $1.14M, and two highly relevant pending listings at $1.15M–$1.175M. However, the June 2026 appraisal at $1.10M, lower portal AVMs, declining ZIP-level prices, abundant active inventory, and frequent price reductions limit the probability of a clean sale materially above list.

The most supportable current sale range is approximately **$1.10M–$1.14M**, with a working midpoint near **$1.12M**. A strong $1.15M–$1.18M result requires excellent showing conversion, clean due-diligence documentation, and either multiple buyers or a buyer who places unusually high value on the creek, decks, and full in-law suite.

This is an analytical range, not an appraisal.

### Versus 2555 Wood Fern

Both provide six bedrooms, five baths, a second kitchen, media room, and multigenerational use. Wood Fern is newer, gated, has a 3-car garage and covered outdoor living. Subject is larger and offers the creek and double decks. Its pending status confirms a buyer pool, while its superior premium features make a subject price materially above $1.15M difficult to justify.

- Somewhat competitive/balanced market.

These are all-price ZIP statistics, not luxury-only metrics. The selected luxury sales show even more price sensitivity: well-priced turnkey properties sold around 99%–100% of list, while aspirational listings absorbed roughly 5%–12% discounts from original ask.

## Pricing conclusion

Do not make an immediate reduction based only on nine active days. First correct syndication errors and review actual showing feedback. If qualified traffic is healthy but offers are absent by approximately 18–21 active days, the issue is probably price-to-condition or risk documentation. If qualified traffic is weak, search positioning and price are the likely causes.

A future reduction should cross a meaningful search/psychology threshold rather than be cosmetic. The two logical levels are approximately **$1.125M** for a measured adjustment or **$1.099M** for a decisive move below the $1.1M threshold. Any decision should be made against the current status of 2815 Waterstone, 1068/2001 Windermere, and the two key pending comps.
