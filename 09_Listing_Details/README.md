# Listing Details Research Hub

Property: 2825 Waterstone Drive, Cumming, GA 30041  
Research date: July 12, 2026

This folder contains the current-listing audit and competitive market analysis requested for the home's active sale.

## Files

1. `01_Current_Listing_Cross_Site_Audit.md` — reconciles the listing across Zillow, Redfin, Realtor.com, Coldwell Banker, Homes.com, and other syndicated sites.
2. `02_Competitive_Market_Analysis.md` — selected active, pending, and recently sold competition in the practical 30041/South Forsyth search area.
3. `03_Salability_Assessment_And_Action_Plan.md` — concise assessment of pricing, buyer appeal, risks, likely timing, and actions.
4. `04_Source_Log.md` — source URLs, access date, and data limitations.
5. `Comparable_Properties_2026-07-12.csv` — structured comp table for future refreshes.
6. `changes.md` — operational tracker and copy-ready requests for MLS, portal, and public-record corrections.

## Geographic interpretation

The requested area was interpreted as the east side of GA-400 in Forsyth County, north of McGinnis Ferry Road and extending north/east toward Lake Lanier. In practice, ZIP 30041 is the primary dataset because it closely matches that buyer search area.

Lakefront estates, large-acreage properties, new construction, and homes with major pool/golf/gated-community premiums are not treated as direct equivalents. They are included only when useful as upper or lower benchmarks.

## Important limitation

This is a portal-based market study, not a broker-only MLS CMA or appraisal. Pending prices are asking prices; the accepted contract prices are not public. Portal status and days-on-market data can lag or conflict. Final pricing decisions should be checked against a fresh FMLS/GAMLS export from the listing agent.
