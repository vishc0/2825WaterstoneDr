## Data cautions

- Portal pages are MLS syndications and may update at different times.
- Search-result crawls and opened-page caches can show different engagement totals or statuses.
- Pending-list prices are not contract prices.
- Square footage sources vary among owner, MLS, appraisal, assessor and portal records.
- Automated valuations do not reliably adjust for finished-basement quality, creek sound, decks, permits, system age, renovation quality, or buyer concessions.
- GreatSchools ratings and assignments can change; verify enrollment directly with Forsyth County Schools.
- The comp set is analytically selected and not a complete broker-only MLS export.
