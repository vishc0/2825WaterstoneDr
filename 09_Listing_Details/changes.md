### County/public record

The public-record section shown by Redfin is separate and currently reports 5 bedrooms, 4 bathrooms and 5,977 sq ft. Do **not** immediately ask the county simply to copy the MLS totals.

First ask the Forsyth County Board of Assessors:

1. Does the 5/4 count represent only above-grade rooms, or the assessor's total recognized rooms?
2. How much finished basement area is currently included in the 5,977 sq ft?
3. What evidence is required to correct bedroom, bathroom, garage and finished-area characteristics?
4. Would the county require an inspection or additional permit review?

A correction can change the assessed characteristics and potentially the taxable value. It may also cause the county to examine the underlying basement finish. The county's official permitting site has separate permit categories for basement finishing, residential renovation and plumbing-only work. Like-for-like kitchen/bath updates are a different issue from whether the original basement finish itself was recognized.

Recommended evidence to assemble before contacting the assessor:

- Current appraisal with area and room breakdown.
- Professional floor plan or measurement report.
- Prior purchase listing showing the basement bedroom, bath and kitchen existed when purchased, if applicable.
- Original builder plans, closing documents or upgrade schedule, if available.
- Photographs showing the lower bedroom, egress, bathroom and finished living areas.
- Receipts describing recent work as like-for-like replacement without relocation of plumbing or structural changes.
- Any permit search result for parcel 176 569.

Forsyth County Board of Assessors: 770-781-2106. The official assessment/appeal page lists `boardofassessors@forsythco.com`. Do not file a formal value appeal merely to ask a property-characteristic question; start with an informational inquiry.
